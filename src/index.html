About me

I enlisted in the US Air Force in January 2016 and separated in January 2022. As I was approaching my separation date, I started contemplating what my life apart from the military looked like. I discovered web development while doing research on my future possibilities. So I enrolled into the Zero to Mastery academy. Since then I have learned how to be a full stack developer, with Javascript, to include React.js and Node.js. I also have learned to work in a collaberative enviroment via git and GitHub.

My interests include value exchanging, networking, and masterminding with like-minded people. I also love hanging out with my wife, son, and two dogs in my freetime. Music is one more thing I'm passionate about, so when I'm not doing any of those things I make my own music.
